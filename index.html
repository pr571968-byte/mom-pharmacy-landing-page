frequently asked questions?

what is mom pharmacy?
Mom Pharmacy stands for Medicine on Minutes — a fast, reliable, and user‑friendly platform that delivers essential medicines and healthcare products within just 10 minutes.
will you really deliver medicines on 10minutes ?
Yes, we aim to deliver within 10 minutes in supported zones with ready stock. Delivery time may vary slightly based on location, traffic, and availability.
what if delivery is delayed ?
If delivery is delayed, our support team will notify you with real-time updates. You can also track your order status or contact us directly for assistance.
how can we place the order ?
You can place the order through our website or mobile app in just a few clicks. Simply upload your prescription, select your items, and proceed to checkout.
what areas do we serve ?
We proudly serve across multiple cities in India, delivering medicines quickly and reliably. Our service areas include urban, suburban, and semi-urban regions for maximum reach.
how can you contact us ?
You can contact us via WhatsApp or by filling out the contact form on our website. We are available 24/7 to assist you with queries.
